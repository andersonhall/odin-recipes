Goop

Home

Description

Goop is a delicious dish served with tortillas.

Ingedients

  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1 onion
  • 1 can Ranch Style beans
  • 1 can Rotel
  • 8 oz. Velveeta
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 1 cup half and half

Steps

  1. Brown ground beef in a skillet with the onion included
  2. Cut Velveeta into 1 inch cubes
  3. In a saucepan, combine Velveeta, taco seasoning, and all canned ingredients
  4. Once beef is brown and onions are soft, drain and then combine with other ingredients in saucepan
  5. Stir until all ingredients are well combined and cheese is fully melted
  6. Add half and half and continue to stir for another minute or two
