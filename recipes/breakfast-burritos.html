Breakfast Burritos

Home

Description

These breakfast burritos are under 300 calories and have 27 grams of protein.

Ingedients

  • 1 turkey sausage patty
  • 1 egg
  • 1/3 cup egg whites
  • 1 tbsp shredded Mexican cheese blend
  • 1 carb friendly wrap

Steps

  1. scramble egg with egg whites
  2. cook turkey sausage patty and slice up into small pieces
  3. combine all ingredients on carb friendly wrap and add back to skillet to seal
